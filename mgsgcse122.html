Data Storage

OCR GCSE Computer Science (J277) - Topic 1.2.4

Overview

This topic covers how different types of data (numbers, characters, images, sound) are represented and stored in binary form within computer systems. It also includes related concepts like hexadecimal notation and binary shifts.
